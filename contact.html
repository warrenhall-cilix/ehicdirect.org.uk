PLEASE NOTE: following the results of the EU referendum, no changes have yet been announced to the European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) service. The NHS Business Services Authority is continuing to provide the EHIC service as usual, and you can apply for and use your card as before. (Source: NHS UK).

Contact EHIC Direct

Please note that whilst applications will still be processed as normal, our support team are off-line until 3rd January when normal service will resume. Please therefore note that general enquiries will not be responded to until the working week commencing 3rd January.

Any requests for cancellation which are submitted via the contact form located on the "Contact us" page of the website will be actioned provided the cancellation request is submitted prior to the application being processed.
