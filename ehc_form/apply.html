APPLY, RENEW OR REPLACE YOUR EHIC CARD USING THE FORM BELOW

Complete the details below to receive your EHIC Card (Formerly E111) via EHIC Direct.

After you have made payment your European Health Insurance Card application will be Verified and processed.

The EHIC will cover any medical treatment that becomes necessary during your trip, for example because of either illness or an accident. (In an emergency you can dial the European emergency number 112 from any telephone or mobile phone).

Important: EHIC Direct is not connected to or affiliated with the Department of Health, the National Health Service, or any other official Government body. We offer a audit and submission service for European Health Insurance Card applications for a small processing fee. Please note that you can apply independently and at no cost for a card by visiting the official NHCBSA website or through the automated EHIC application service on 0845 606 2030.
