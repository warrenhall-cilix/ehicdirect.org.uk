The EHIC (European Health Insurance Card) replaced the E111 form which was phased out early in 2006. The EHIC entitles the holder to state-funded (or free) medical care, in case of any emergency medical situation, whilst they are temporarily located in any EU country Including Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway and Switzerland. Apply now and leave the rest to us.

We offer an online verification and audit service for the European Health Insurance Card. We will make sure that your EHIC application is processed correctly and that all necessary information is in place.
In the case of mistakes or insufficient information in your EHIC application form we will contact you and make sure that your EHIC application has all the necessary information required.

We also offer an auto-renew service that enables you to travel worry free knowing your EHIC is always valid and that all your details are up to date. We will remind you that your EHIC card is about to expire and renew your card.
You can apply for the EHIC free of charge at the NHSBSA website (www.ehic.org.uk). Unlike the NHS website, our online form is available 24 hours 365 days a year and every application will be checked manually.

Our team of experts will make sure that your application is filled in properly and verify all your details. In the case of any spelling or other common mistakes we will fix them and make sure your application is filled correctly.

Please allow up to 7-10 working days for your card(s) to arrive. Applying for express processing means that your application will be prioritized, however, the actual delivery time is dependent upon the NHS and Royal Mail's delivery service.

Important: EHIC Direct is not connected to or affiliated with the Department of Health, the National Health Service, or any other official Government body. We offer a audit and submission service for European Health Insurance Card applications for a small processing fee. Please note that you can apply independently and at no cost for a card by visiting the official NHCBSA website or through the automated EHIC application service on 0845 606 2030.
